# Project Knowledge

## Positioning

Turn your music into scroll-stopping visuals — fast.

Short, audio-aware visuals for creators who post music often.

## Who It’s For

- Independent musicians
- TikTok / Reels creators
- Producers & DJs
- Social media managers for artists

## What Makes It Different

1. Built for short-form, not full videos
   - 8–15 second hooks
   - Loopable visuals
   - Chorus-focused clips

2. Audio-aware by default
   - Beat intensity
   - Drops
   - Chorus timing
   - Lyric moments

3. Generate variants, not guesses
   - Multiple interpretations for the same moment
   - Quick side-by-side comparison

4. Fixed outcomes, predictable cost
   - Known length, style, and credit cost per generation

5. Loop-ready visuals
   - Clean loops for Canvas, TikTok intros, Reels hooks

## Core Features

- Upload audio (song, demo, or snippet)
- Optional lyrics or chorus markers
- Choose outcome preset (Hook, Canvas, Highlight)
- Generate multiple variants at once
- Preview and compare side-by-side
- Save favorites
- Export ready-to-post clips

## Focus (and Non-Goals)

Focus:
- Speed
- Iteration
- Short-form performance
- Repeatable results

Not focusing on:
- Promising virality
- Replacing human creativity
- 3-minute music videos
- Overwhelming users with controls

## Product Notes

- Emphasis on consistency: help creators show up often.
- Clip lengths are short by design; prioritize fast turnaround.
- Pricing should map to fixed outcomes for transparency.
